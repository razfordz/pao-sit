## Project Overview

This project is a GovTech AI platform called “เป๋าสิทธิ์” (Pao-Sit).

“เป๋าสิทธิ์” is an AI-powered welfare assistant designed to help citizens discover, understand, and access government benefits, welfare programs, and public services more easily using AI, Open Government Data, and ThaiLLM for personalized recommendations and eligibility analysis.

System goals:
- Help citizens understand what benefits they are eligible for
- Reduce the complexity of bureaucratic/government language
- Simplify access to welfare and public services
- Make government services more accessible for all age groups

The platform should feel:
- friendly
- easy to understand
- modern
- trustworthy
- accessible for everyone

The platform should NOT feel:
- like traditional government software
- overly complicated
- too technical
- cyberpunk or futuristic

## Product Positioning Direction

The product positioning has evolved from a simple welfare search platform into a proactive AI civic assistant.

Core narrative:
“กรุงเทพรู้ใจคุณ”
“A city that understands you before you have to search for help yourself.”

The platform should communicate:
- proactive assistance
- personalized civic support
- AI-powered benefit matching
- human-centered government experience

Avoid positioning the platform as:
- a search engine
- a directory of benefits
- a traditional government portal
- a static information dashboard

Instead, the experience should feel like:
- the city understands your life situation
- AI helps discover benefits automatically
- support comes to citizens proactively
- government services become personalized

Landing page messaging should emphasize:
- “เมืองที่เข้าใจคุณ ก่อนที่คุณจะต้องค้นหาเอง”
- “สิทธิ์ที่ควรได้ ไม่ควรต้องหาเอง”
- “AI ช่วยจับคู่สิทธิ์ บริการ และโอกาสที่เหมาะกับชีวิตคุณ”

The emotional direction should feel:
- hopeful
- caring
- intelligent
- trustworthy
- empowering

The UX should create the feeling that:
“Bangkok understands me.”
instead of:
“I have to figure everything out myself.”

## AI Interaction Style

The AI Assistant should feel:
- polite
- friendly
- warm
- easy to understand
- conversational
- like a personal assistant

Use simple conversational Thai instead of formal bureaucratic language.

Example:

Instead of:
“ดำเนินการยื่นคำร้อง”

Use:
“สมัครสิทธิ์”
